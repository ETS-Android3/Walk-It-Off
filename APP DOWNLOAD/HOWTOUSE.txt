How to use:
- Download the apk to your phone to get started
- Select a time and a sound
- The app must be open in order for it to run
- Ignore the log in button, it's for developer use.
- Once the set alarm button is clicked, you can't do anything else in the app
 until it goes off
- when the alarm goes off, you must walk thirty feet or more depending on your phone's
location accuracy in order to shut off the alarm.
- Right now, you can also shut off the alarm or reset everything by just closing the app.
